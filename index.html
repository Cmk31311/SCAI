Built for developers

Turn whatever is on your screen into something you can talk to: errors, logs, dashboards, docs, or any UI.

Global hotkey capture

Press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + X from anywhere to capture your screen and run OCR instantly.

Interactive Q&A chat

Ask questions about your screen and get grounded answers using your capture as context.

Code-aware debugging

Automatic error detection with root cause analysis and fix snippets you can paste directly.

How it works

1

Capture your screen

Press the global hotkey. ScreenAI captures your screen and renders it to a canvas for processing.

2

Local OCR

Text is extracted locally using Tesseract.js. Results appear instantly in the app.

3

Ask questions

Redacted text is sent to AI. Get explanations, summaries, and code fixes in seconds.

Privacy & security first

Local OCR

OCR runs on your machine. Only the cleaned-up text is sent to the AI API.

Redacted by default

ScreenAI automatically scrubs emails, tokens, and private keys before sending anything over the wire.

Transparent by design

See exactly what's being sent in the request so you stay in control of your data.
